3-Way Reconciliation

Every single rupee matched. Zero data leakage.

Our platform matches order payloads from Amazon, Flipkart, and Shopify directly to payout settlements from payment gateways (Razorpay, PayU, Stripe) and actual deposits verified in bank accounts. Continuous 3-way validation prevents common tax leaks and order tracking failures.

  • Maker-Checker validation parameters on all items
  • Automatic fee verification checks to catch hidden gateway charges
  • Audit trails locked with secure SHA-256 validation hashes

Immutable Ledger Compliance

Every autonomous transaction update is cryptographically hashed with SHA-256 and stored on private ledger systems. Full SOC 2 alignment ensures data integrity, masking PII records before they ever touch indexing pipelines.

Maker-Checker Governance Controls

No agent posts unilaterally to your production ERP ledger. Automated agents acts as the "Maker," assembling payouts. A licensed Chartered Accountant acts as the "Checker," verifying and approving records before posting.
